About

NextSoccerDerby provides a simple, compact and easy-to-navigate place to find dates of upcoming spectacular clashes between rival soccer teams.

Tracking derbies from all around the world, ranging from the notorious El Clásico, through Derby d'Italia and Derby della Madonnina in Italy, a tense German Der Klassiker, prestige city battles North London derby and Manchester derby in English Premier League, to The Intercontinental Derby and Superclásico in Argentina, NextSoccerDerby helps you to keep track of all the must-watch match-ups.


Please, grab a snack and have a seat. It's derby time!
